About Me

An aspiring web developer committed to making a significant and positive impact wheresoever I go.

I was born and raised in Elmhurst, a suburb ~20 miles west of Chicago, where I also attended college. After graduating from Elmhurst College in 2016 with a degree in Finance and Economics, I realized a career in Finance was not for me and accepted a Marketing internship at a startup. After much soul-searching, I recognized my skills lie in logic and creativity. *Cue Google search: "careers that use logic and creativity"* Fast forward to today and I am so happy I now get to call the beautiful city of Chicago my home and attend Northwestern University's Coding Bootcamp to help jumpstart my career as a web developer!

Aside from coding, you can find me drinking coffee, enjoying the outdoors (except in Chicago winters...brrr), shooting portrait photography, practicing Muay Thai kickboxing, cooking comfort food, and listening to podcasts/reading books about Self-Development, Psychology, Spirituality, and Business.
